Biryani

Description

Biryani is a layered Indian dish that features rice combined with meat—such as lamb, chicken, or fish—or vegetables, all cooked in a rich gravy infused with spices like saffron. It is considered a special occasion dish, often associated with celebrations and festive gatherings across India and beyond.
